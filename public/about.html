About Us

Our History

Addis Zemen Preparatory School was established in 1990 with the mission of providing quality education to the youth of our community. Over the years, the school has grown and developed, maintaining its commitment to excellence and discipline.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to develop their potential and contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading preparatory school recognized for academic excellence and character development.
